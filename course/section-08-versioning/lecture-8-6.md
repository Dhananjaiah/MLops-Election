## Real-World Context

> **Story**: The GDPR Fine for Untracked Model Updates
>
> A European bank was fined €2M because they couldn't prove who deployed a model that was making biased loan decisions. They had no audit trail of who promoted the model to production or approved it. Proper governance would have prevented this.
