## Real-World Context

> **Story**: The 3 AM Alert Nobody Wanted
>
> A pipeline was scheduled to run at 3 AM. When it failed, it paged the on-call engineer. The fix? Add 3 retries with exponential backoff—95% of failures were transient network issues that resolved in 30 seconds. Alerts dropped by 90%.
